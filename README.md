Project Structure:
Dataset Requirements:

Flights: Contains flight schedules, routes, and operational details.
Bookings: Records customer bookings, cancellations, and payments.
Airports: Details about airport codes, names, and locations.
Customer Feedback: Logs customer complaints and feedback.
Key Analyses:

Identify busiest routes (based on bookings).
Track ticket sales trends over time.
Analyze the impact of delays on customer satisfaction.
Determine complaint patterns and suggest improvements.
